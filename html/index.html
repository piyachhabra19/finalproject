How to Play

1. Press the "Roll Dice" button to roll the dice.

2. Both you and the computer roll two dice.

3. Your score for each round is calculated based on the dice values.

4. After 3 rounds, the winner is announced.

Scoring Rules

1. Rolling a 1 in any die gives a score of 0 for that round.

2. Rolling a pair (e.g., 5 and 5) gives double the total: (5+5)*2=20.

3. Other combinations add up to the total of the two dice (e.g., 3+2=5).
